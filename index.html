With a team of political and policy experts spanning key European capitals and both sides of the Atlantic, Rasmussen Global is Europe’s preeminent independent geopolitical advisory firm.

A FILM WITH A PURPOSE

Founded in 2014 by former NATO Secretary General and Danish Prime Minister Anders Fogh Rasmussen, we are a firm with a clear purpose: to help companies and democratic governments navigate international politics, shape public policy, and get their message across.

Our global team has decades of experience at the top of governments, international institutions, and multinational companies across Europe and North America—and we deliver results.
